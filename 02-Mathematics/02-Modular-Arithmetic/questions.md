## Question 1: Super Pow

- **Link:** https://leetcode.com/problems/super-pow/
- **Difficulty:** Medium
- **Description:** Calculate a^b mod 1337 where b is a very large number represented as an array.

- **Why Interview-Worthy:** Tests modular arithmetic with large exponents.
