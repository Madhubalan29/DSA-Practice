# 🔥 Interview & CP Questions — Combinatorics

## Question 1: Unique Paths
- **Platform:** LeetCode
- **Link:** https://leetcode.com/problems/unique-paths/
- **Difficulty:** Medium
- **Description:** Count unique paths from top-left to bottom-right of an m×n grid.
- **Key Concepts:** Combinatorics, DP
- **Why Interview-Worthy:** Direct application of C(m+n-2, m-1).
