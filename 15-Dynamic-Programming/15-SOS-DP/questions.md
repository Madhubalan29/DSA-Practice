# 🔥 Interview & CP Questions — SOS DP

## Question 1: Sum over Subsets (SOS) DP
- **Platform:** Codeforces
- **Link:** https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/45223
- **Difficulty:** Hard
- **Description:** For each mask, compute sum of f(submask) over all submasks.
- **Key Concepts:** SOS DP, Bitmask
- **Why Interview-Worthy:** Optimizes O(3^n) to O(n * 2^n).
