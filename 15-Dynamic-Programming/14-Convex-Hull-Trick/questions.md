# 🔥 Interview & CP Questions — Convex Hull Trick

## Question 1: Convex Hull Trick
- **Platform:** CP-Algorithms
- **Link:** https://cp-algorithms.com/geometry/convex_hull_trick.html
- **Difficulty:** Hard
- **Description:** Optimize DP transitions of form dp[i] = min(dp[j] + b[j]*a[i]).
- **Key Concepts:** Convex Hull Trick
- **Why Interview-Worthy:** Optimizes certain DP recurrences from O(n²) to O(n log n).
