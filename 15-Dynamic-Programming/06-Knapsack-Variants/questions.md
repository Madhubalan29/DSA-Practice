## Question 2: Partition Equal Subset Sum
- **Platform:** LeetCode
- **Link:** https://leetcode.com/problems/partition-equal-subset-sum/
- **Difficulty:** Medium
- **Description:** Can array be partitioned into two equal-sum subsets?
- **Key Concepts:** 0/1 Knapsack
- **Why Interview-Worthy:** Subset sum = target/2.

## Question 3: Target Sum
- **Platform:** LeetCode
- **Link:** https://leetcode.com/problems/target-sum/
