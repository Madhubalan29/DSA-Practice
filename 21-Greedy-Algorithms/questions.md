# 🔥 Interview Hard Questions — Greedy Algorithms

## Question 1: Candy
- **Platform:** LeetCode
- **Link:** https://leetcode.com/problems/candy/
- **Difficulty:** Hard
- **Description:** There are n children standing in a line with ratings. Each child must have at least one candy. Children with a higher rating than their neighbors must get more candies. Minimize total candies.
- **Key Concepts:** Two-pass Greedy, Left-to-right and Right-to-left scans
- **Why Interview-Worthy:** Classic greedy hard problem — tests bidirectional constraint satisfaction.

## Question 2: IPO (Initial Public Offering)
- **Platform:** LeetCode
- **Link:** https://leetcode.com/problems/ipo/
- **Difficulty:** Hard
- **Description:** Given k projects to select, each with profit and capital requirement, start with capital w. Choose projects to maximize final capital. Can do at most k projects.
- **Key Concepts:** Greedy + Two Heaps (min-heap for capital, max-heap for profits)
- **Why Interview-Worthy:** Elegant two-heap greedy pattern — frequently asked at Amazon, Goldman Sachs.
