# 🔥 Interview & CP Questions — Ad Hoc

## Question 1: Watermelon
- **Platform:** Codeforces
- **Link:** https://codeforces.com/problemset/problem/4/A
- **Difficulty:** Easy
- **Description:** Determine if a watermelon of weight w can be divided into two even parts.
- **Key Concepts:** Ad-hoc, Math
- **Why Interview-Worthy:** Classic beginner problem that tests edge case thinking.

- **Platform:** LeetCode
- **Link:** https://leetcode.com/problems/next-permutation/
- **Difficulty:** Medium
- **Description:** Implement next permutation, which rearranges numbers into the lexicographically next greater permutation.
- **Key Concepts:** Ad-hoc, Arrays
- **Why Interview-Worthy:** Frequently asked in interviews; tests in-place algorithmic thinking.
