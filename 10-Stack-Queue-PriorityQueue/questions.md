# 🔥 Interview Hard Questions — Stack, Queue, Priority Queue

## Question 1: Largest Rectangle in Histogram
- **Platform:** LeetCode
- **Link:** https://leetcode.com/problems/largest-rectangle-in-histogram/
- **Difficulty:** Hard
- **Description:** Given an array of integers heights representing the histogram's bar heights, find the area of the largest rectangle in the histogram.
- **Key Concepts:** Monotonic Stack, Next Smaller Element, Area computation
- **Why Interview-Worthy:** Fundamental monotonic stack problem — basis for many other hard problems.

## Question 2: Trapping Rain Water
- **Platform:** LeetCode
- **Link:** https://leetcode.com/problems/trapping-rain-water/
- **Difficulty:** Hard
- **Description:** Given n non-negative integers representing an elevation map, compute how much water it can trap after raining.
- **Key Concepts:** Stack-based approach, Two Pointers, Prefix Max
- **Why Interview-Worthy:** One of the most frequently asked hard problems across all top companies.
