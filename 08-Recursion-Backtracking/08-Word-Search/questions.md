# 🔥 Interview & CP Questions — Word Search

## Question 1: Word Search
- **Platform:** LeetCode
- **Link:** https://leetcode.com/problems/word-search/
- **Difficulty:** Medium
- **Description:** Determine if the word exists in the grid.
- **Key Concepts:** Backtracking
- **Why Interview-Worthy:** Classic grid backtracking — one of the most asked mediums.

- **Description:** Find all words in the board that are in the dictionary.
- **Key Concepts:** Backtracking, Trie
- **Why Interview-Worthy:** Trie + backtracking optimization — top hard problem.
