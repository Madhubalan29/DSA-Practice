# 🔥 Interview & CP Questions — Rat In Maze

## Question 1: Rat in a Maze Problem
- **Platform:** GFG
- **Link:** https://practice.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/rat-in-a-maze-problem/1
- **Difficulty:** Medium
- **Description:** Find all paths for a rat from (0,0) to (n-1,n-1) in a maze.
- **Key Concepts:** Backtracking
- **Why Interview-Worthy:** Classic backtracking on grid.

## Question 3: The Maze
- **Platform:** LeetCode
- **Link:** https://leetcode.com/problems/the-maze/
